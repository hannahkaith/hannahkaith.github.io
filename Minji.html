Let's all give it up for our teddy bear member of the group, Kim Minji (김민지)!

Minji was born on the 7th of May 2004, making her 19. She was born in South Korea where she grew up. During her elementary school years, she acquired English skills in preparation for studying abroad in Canada.

It is Minji's nature to be quite introverted. A few of her favoured pastimes are reading, going for walks, watching movies, and writing and decorating her diary. When it comes to books, Winnie the Pooh is a favourite, but she enjoys reading in general, particularly mystery novels. The way she portrays herself on camera, she is a serene individual who never loses sight of the positive side of things.
