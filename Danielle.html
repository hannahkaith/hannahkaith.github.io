Say hello to our sunshine member of the group, Danielle Marsh!

By her Korean name, she is Mo Jihye (모지혜). Danielle was born on the 11th of April 2005, making her 18. She was born in Australia, then moved to South Korea until 2012. In 2019, she went back to Korea to become a trainee.

Danielle is an outdoorsy type of person. One day, she hopes to start a surfing club with her fellow member Hanni, as she loves surfing! Besides that, some of Danielle's favourite things involve swimming, art, animals, shopping, and watching sunsets. It is evident that she is a character who, when given the chance, will express herself creatively.
